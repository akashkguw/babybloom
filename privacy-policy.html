BabyBloom Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Overview

BabyBloom ("the App") is a baby care companion designed to help parents track feeding, sleep, diapers, milestones, and more. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Storage

All data you enter into BabyBloom is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (IndexedDB). This includes:

  • Baby profiles and birth dates
  • Feeding, diaper, sleep, and activity logs
  • Milestone and vaccination tracking
  • Growth measurements and health notes
  • Emergency contacts
  • App preferences and settings

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it (for example, using the Partner Sync or Report features, which operate via direct peer-to-peer sharing under your control).

Data Collection

BabyBloom does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not use advertising SDKs
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not require an account or login
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or location

Permissions

BabyBloom may request the following device permissions, all of which are optional:

  • Notifications: To send feeding reminders that you configure. No data is sent externally.
  • Microphone: For the voice logging feature, which processes speech on-device. Audio is not recorded or stored.
  • Speech Recognition: Used alongside the microphone for voice commands. Processing occurs on-device.

You can deny any permission and the app will continue to function normally without those features.

Third-Party Services

BabyBloom loads the Nunito font from Google Fonts. This is the only external network request the app makes. Google's privacy policy for this service is available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

BabyBloom is designed for parents and caregivers, not for use by children. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The baby-related data entered by parents is stored only on their own device.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it at all times. To delete your data:

  • Use the Settings screen within the app to clear specific data
  • Uninstall the app to remove all stored data
  • Clear the app's storage through your device's system settings

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, biometric lock). Since data is stored only on-device and is never transmitted over the internet, it cannot be intercepted or accessed remotely.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
